For a 12 mm deep lesion, “no localization” risks conversion or excessive resection.
Why others are incorrect: (a) Hookwire dislodgement risk and lack of adjunctive imaging undermine reliability. (b) Lipiodol needs fluoroscopy; not useful here. (d) Microcoils require fluoroscopy to visualize. (e) Small, deep nodules are often impalpable; localization improves success and limits margins.
